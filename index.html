Key Features and Benefits

The Optima CT520 comes with a wide array of premium features that make it stand out in its class:

1. 70cm Aperture with Laser Positioning

Comfortable access for patients of all sizes, and precise laser positioning for accurate alignment every time.

2. Solid-State HiLight Matrix II Detector

This advanced detector improves spatial resolution while reducing noise. You get clearer, more consistent images, even at lower doses.

3. Advanced 3D Applications and Multi-Planar Reformatting

View detailed anatomical structures from any angle. Perfect for surgical planning, trauma cases, or complex diagnostic evaluations.

4. HyperPlane Helical Reconstruction

Enhances image sharpness and clarity across all planes, allowing for confident diagnoses in less time.

5. CrossBeam Correction

Reduces artifacts and distortion, resulting in cleaner images that help minimize repeat scans.

6. OptiDose Technology

Designed to minimize radiation exposure without compromising diagnostic value. A critical feature in pediatric care and repeat-scan situations.

This CT scanner doesn’t just scan—it works smarter, faster, and safer for both your staff and your patients.
